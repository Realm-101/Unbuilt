## Running Gap Analysis Searches

### Starting a New Search

1. Click "New Search" or press `Ctrl/Cmd + N`
2. Enter your search query in natural language
3. (Optional) Add to an existing project or create a new one
4. Click "Analyze" to start

### Writing Effective Search Queries

**Good Examples:**
- "Gaps in sustainable packaging for e-commerce"
- "Untapped opportunities in remote healthcare for seniors"
- "What's missing in the project management software market?"
- "Innovation opportunities in electric vehicle charging infrastructure"

**Tips:**
- Be specific about the industry or market
- Mention target audience if relevant
- Include geographic focus if applicable
- Ask about specific pain points or needs

### What Happens During Analysis

The AI performs a multi-phase analysis:

1. **Market Research** (30-45 seconds)
   - Searches current market landscape
   - Identifies existing solutions
   - Analyzes competitor offerings

2. **Gap Identification** (45-60 seconds)
   - Discovers unmet needs
   - Identifies underserved segments
   - Finds innovation opportunities

3. **Feasibility Assessment** (30-45 seconds)
   - Evaluates technical feasibility
   - Assesses market readiness
   - Estimates resource requirements

4. **Report Generation** (15-30 seconds)
   - Compiles comprehensive insights
   - Generates innovation scores
   - Creates actionable recommendations

**Total time:** 2-3 minutes per search

## Action Plans & Progress Tracking

### Understanding Action Plans

Every gap analysis includes a customizable 4-phase development roadmap. The AI generates a comprehensive plan based on your opportunity, which you can then adapt to your specific needs and track your progress as you execute.

### Phase Structure

**Phase 1: Research & Validation (Months 1-3)**
- Market research and validation
- Customer interviews
- Competitive analysis
- Feasibility assessment

**Phase 2: MVP Development (Months 4-6)**
- Product design and development
- Core feature implementation
- Initial testing
- Feedback collection

**Phase 3: Market Testing (Months 7-9)**
- Beta launch
- User acquisition
- Metrics tracking
- Iteration based on feedback

**Phase 4: Scale & Growth (Months 10-12)**
- Full market launch
- Marketing and sales
- Team expansion
- Process optimization

### Customizing Your Action Plan

#### Adding Custom Tasks

1. Navigate to any phase in your action plan
2. Click the **"Add Task"** button
3. Fill in the task details:
   - **Title** - Clear, actionable task name (required)
   - **Description** - Detailed explanation of what needs to be done
   - **Estimated Time** - How long you expect this to take
   - **Resources** - Links, tools, or materials needed
4. Click **"Create Task"** to add it to your plan

**Tips for Custom Tasks:**
- Keep titles action-oriented (e.g., "Interview 10 potential customers")
- Include specific deliverables in descriptions
- Be realistic with time estimates
- Link to relevant resources from the Resource Library

#### Editing Tasks

1. Click on any task to view its details
2. Click the **"Edit"** button (pencil icon)
3. Modify any field:
   - Title, description, estimated time, resources
   - Task status (Not Started, In Progress, Completed, Skipped)
4. Changes save automatically

**What You Can Edit:**
- ‚úÖ Task titles and descriptions
- ‚úÖ Time estimates and resources
- ‚úÖ Task order within a phase
- ‚úÖ Task status
- ‚ùå The phase a task belongs to (delete and recreate instead)

#### Deleting Tasks

1. Click on the task you want to remove
2. Click the **"Delete"** button (trash icon)
3. Confirm deletion

**Note:** Deleting a task is permanent. If you might need it later, consider marking it as "Skipped" instead.

### Tips & Best Practices

**Getting Started:**
- Review the AI-generated plan first before customizing
- Start with the template closest to your project type
- Don't over-customize initially - adjust as you learn

**Task Management:**
- Break large tasks into smaller, actionable steps
- Set realistic time estimates (add buffer time)
- Update task status regularly to maintain momentum
- Use dependencies to enforce logical order

**Progress Tracking:**
- Check off tasks as soon as you complete them
- Review progress weekly to stay on track
- Celebrate phase completions to maintain motivation
- Adjust estimates based on actual time taken

**Customization:**
- Add tasks for your specific situation
- Skip tasks that don't apply to you
- Reorder based on your priorities and constraints
- Use templates as starting points, not rigid rules

**Collaboration (Pro/Enterprise):**
- Assign tasks to team members
- Use comments to discuss approach
- Share progress updates regularly
- Export to your team's project management tool

### Troubleshooting

**Tasks won't reorder?**
- Make sure you're dragging by the handle (‚ãÆ‚ãÆ)
- Check that you have edit permissions
- Try refreshing the page

**Progress not saving?**
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you're logged in (not guest mode)
- Disable browser extensions that might interfere
- Clear cache and reload

**Can't add dependencies?**
- Verify both tasks are in the same plan
- Check for circular dependencies
- Ensure prerequisite task exists

**Export not working?**
- Check your browser's download settings
- Disable popup blockers
- Try a different export format
- Contact support if issue persists
