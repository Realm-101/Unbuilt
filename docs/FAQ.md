# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is Unbuilt?
Unbuilt is an AI-powered platform that helps you discover untapped market opportunities and innovation gaps. We analyze industries and markets to identify what doesn't exist yet, providing detailed insights about feasibility, market potential, and actionable development plans.

### Who is Unbuilt for?
Unbuilt is designed for:
- **Entrepreneurs** seeking new business opportunities
- **Investors** looking for emerging trends
- **Product Managers** researching market white space
- **Researchers** conducting gap analysis
- **Innovation Teams** generating R&D ideas

### How much does Unbuilt cost?
- **Free Tier**: 5 searches per month, basic features
- **Pro Tier**: Unlimited searches, advanced features, priority processing
- **Enterprise Tier**: Custom solutions, dedicated support, team management

## Onboarding & Getting Started

### Do I need to complete the onboarding tour?
No, the onboarding tour is optional. You can skip it and start using Unbuilt immediately. You can always resume the tour later from the Help menu.

### Can I change my role after onboarding?
Yes! Go to Settings > Profile and update your role. This will adjust which features and resources are highlighted for you.

### What happens if I skip the interactive tour?
Nothing! You can resume the tour anytime from Help > Resume Tour. The tour is designed to help you discover features, but you can explore on your own.

### How do I restart the onboarding process?
Go to Settings > Account > Reset Onboarding. This will clear your onboarding progress and let you start fresh.

## Dashboard & Organization

### How do I organize my searches?
You can organize searches in several ways:
1. **Favorites**: Star important analyses for quick access
2. **Projects**: Group related analyses into projects
3. **Tags**: Add custom tags for filtering
4. **Filters**: Sort by date, score, or tags

### What are Projects?
Projects let you group related gap analyses together. For example, you might create a "Healthcare Innovation" project and add all your healthcare-related searches to it.

### How do I create a Project?
1. Go to your Dashboard
2. Click "New Project" in the Projects section
3. Enter a name and description
4. Drag and drop analyses into the project, or assign them from the analysis page

### Can I move analyses between projects?
Yes! Simply drag and drop search cards between projects on your dashboard, or use the "Assign to Project" option on any analysis page.

### How many projects can I create?
- **Free Tier**: Up to 3 projects
- **Pro Tier**: Unlimited projects
- **Enterprise Tier**: Unlimited projects with team sharing

## Action Plans & Progress Tracking

### What is an Action Plan?
Every gap analysis includes a 4-phase development roadmap:
1. **Validation Phase**: Verify market demand
2. **Planning Phase**: Define your strategy
3. **Development Phase**: Build your solution
4. **Launch Phase**: Go to market

### How do I track my progress?
Click the checkbox next to each step as you complete it. Your progress is automatically saved and synced across devices.

### Can I undo a checked step?
Yes! Click the checkbox again to uncheck it. You can also use the undo button that appears briefly after checking a step.

### What happens when I complete a phase?
You'll see a celebration animation (confetti!) and a congratulatory message. The next phase will be unlocked for you to continue.

### Can I customize the action plan steps?
Yes! You have full control over your action plans:
- **Add custom tasks** to any phase
- **Edit task details** (title, description, time estimates, resources)
- **Delete or skip tasks** that don't apply
- **Reorder tasks** with drag-and-drop
- **Set task dependencies** to enforce order
- **Apply templates** for different project types
- **Export to other tools** (Trello, Asana, CSV, Markdown)

See the [User Guide](./USER_GUIDE.md#action-plans--progress-tracking) for detailed instructions.

### Does my progress sync across devices?
Yes! Your progress is saved to your account and syncs automatically across all your devices.

## Sharing & Collaboration

### How do I share an analysis?
1. Open any analysis
2. Click the "Share" button
3. Generate a secure link
4. Copy and send to anyone

### Do people need an account to view shared links?
No! Shared links are read-only and don't require recipients to create an account.

### Can I set an expiration date for shared links?
Yes! When creating a share link, you can set an expiration date. After that date, the link will no longer work.

### How do I revoke a shared link?
Go to Settings > Shared Links, find the link you want to revoke, and click "Revoke Access". The link will immediately stop working.

### Can I see who viewed my shared link?
Yes! Each shared link shows the number of views and the last access time. Pro and Enterprise users get detailed analytics including viewer locations and devices.

### Can I collaborate with others on an analysis?
Currently, sharing is read-only. Real-time collaboration features are coming in a future update for Pro and Enterprise users.

## Navigation & Search

### What is Global Search?
Global Search (Ctrl/Cmd + K) lets you quickly find anything in Unbuilt:
- Your analyses
- Help articles
- Resources
- Pages and features

### How do I use keyboard shortcuts?
Press `?` to see all available keyboard shortcuts. You can customize shortcuts in Settings > Keyboard Shortcuts.

### What are the most useful shortcuts?
- `Ctrl/Cmd + K` - Global search
- `Ctrl/Cmd + N` - New search
- `Ctrl/Cmd + D` - Go to dashboard
- `Ctrl/Cmd + E` - Export current analysis
- `?` - Show all shortcuts

### Can I customize keyboard shortcuts?
Yes! Go to Settings > Keyboard Shortcuts to rebind any shortcut to your preference.

### Why don't shortcuts work in input fields?
This is intentional to prevent conflicts. Shortcuts are disabled when you're typing in text fields, search boxes, or text areas.

## Accessibility

### Is Unbuilt accessible?
Yes! Unbuilt meets WCAG 2.1 Level AA accessibility standards:
- Full keyboard navigation
- Screen reader support
- High contrast mode
- Reduced motion option
- Resizable text

### How do I enable accessibility features?
Go to Settings > Accessibility to enable:
- High Contrast Mode
- Reduced Motion
- Screen Reader Optimized Mode

### Can I navigate without a mouse?
Yes! All features are fully keyboard accessible. Use Tab to move between elements, Enter/Space to activate, and arrow keys for navigation.

### Does Unbuilt work with screen readers?
Yes! We've tested with NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver. Enable "Screen Reader Optimized Mode" in Settings for the best experience.

## Technical Questions

### What browsers are supported?
- Chrome 90+
- Firefox 88+
- Safari 14+
- Edge 90+

Internet Explorer is not supported.

### Why isn't my progress saving?
Check that:
1. You're logged in (not guest mode)
2. Cookies and local storage are enabled
3. You're not in private/incognito mode
4. Your internet connection is stable

### How do I clear my cache?
In your browser:
- Chrome: Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Delete
- Firefox: Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Delete
- Safari: Cmd + Option + E

### Does Unbuilt work offline?
Limited offline support is available. You can view previously loaded analyses, but you can't run new searches or sync progress without an internet connection.
