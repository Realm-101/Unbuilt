# Getting Started with Unbuilt

Welcome to Unbuilt! This guide will help you get started with discovering untapped market opportunities using our AI-powered innovation gap analysis platform.

## What is Unbuilt?

Unbuilt helps entrepreneurs, startups, and innovators discover what doesn't exist yet. Our platform analyzes industries and markets to identify gaps and opportunities, providing detailed insights about feasibility, market potential, and actionable development roadmaps.

## Quick Start

### 1. Create Your Account

Visit [unbuilt.one](https://unbuilt.one) and sign up with your email address. You'll start with a Free tier account that includes 5 searches per month.

### 2. Complete Onboarding

When you first log in, you'll be guided through a personalized onboarding experience:

1. **Select Your Role**: Choose what brings you to Unbuilt:
   - Entrepreneur
   - Investor
   - Product Manager
   - Researcher
   - Just Exploring

2. **Take the Interactive Tour**: Learn about key features through step-by-step guidance. You can skip this and resume it later from the Help menu.

### 3. Run Your First Search

1. Click "New Search" or use the keyboard shortcut `Ctrl/Cmd + N`
2. Enter your innovation idea or market area
3. Wait for the AI analysis (typically 30-60 seconds)
4. Review your comprehensive gap analysis results

## Understanding Your Dashboard

Your dashboard is your command center for managing all your innovation explorations:

### Recent Searches
- View your 5 most recent analyses
- Quick actions: View, Export, Delete, Favorite
- Click "View All" to see your complete search history

### Favorites
- Star analyses you want to keep handy
- Quickly access your most important discoveries
- Sort by date or innovation score

### Projects
- Organize related analyses into projects
- Track progress across multiple ideas
- Add descriptions and tags for easy organization

### Quick Stats
- See your search usage (Free tier: X of 5 remaining)
- Track your favorites count
- Monitor active projects

## Key Features

### Progressive Disclosure
Information is revealed gradually to prevent overwhelm:
- **Summary View**: See key metrics at a glance
- **Expandable Sections**: Click to reveal detailed analysis
- **Tabbed Content**: Organize complex information into digestible tabs

### Action Plan Tracking
Every analysis includes a 4-phase development roadmap:
1. **Validation Phase**: Verify market demand
2. **Planning Phase**: Define your strategy
3. **Development Phase**: Build your solution
4. **Launch Phase**: Go to market

Check off steps as you complete them to track your progress!

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Speed up your workflow with these shortcuts:

- `Ctrl/Cmd + K` - Global search
- `Ctrl/Cmd + N` - New search
- `Ctrl/Cmd + D` - Go to dashboard
- `Ctrl/Cmd + E` - Export current analysis
- `?` - Show all shortcuts
- `Esc` - Close modals

Customize shortcuts in Settings > Keyboard Shortcuts.
