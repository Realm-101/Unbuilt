# Unbuilt UX Features Guide

## Getting Started

Welcome to Unbuilt! This guide will help you understand and use all the powerful features available to help you discover market gaps and innovation opportunities.

### First Time Users

When you first sign up for Unbuilt, you'll be guided through a personalized onboarding experience:

1. **Role Selection**: Tell us what brings you to Unbuilt (Entrepreneur, Investor, Product Manager, Researcher, or Just Exploring)
2. **Interactive Tour**: Take a guided tour of key features tailored to your role
3. **Dashboard Overview**: Learn how to navigate your workspace

You can skip the onboarding at any time and restart it later from **Account Settings > Onboarding**.

---

## Dashboard

Your dashboard is your command center for managing all your innovation analyses.

### Quick Stats
At the top of your dashboard, you'll see:
- **Searches Used**: Track your monthly search usage
- **Favorites**: Number of analyses you've favorited
- **Active Projects**: Count of projects you're working on

### Recent Searches
View your 5 most recent gap analyses with:
- Innovation score and feasibility rating
- Quick actions (View, Export, Delete, Favorite)
- Click "View All" to see your complete search history

### Favorites
Access your favorited analyses quickly:
- Toggle favorites with the star icon
- Sort by date or innovation score
- Organize your best discoveries

### Projects
Organize multiple analyses into projects:
- Create projects with names and descriptions
- Drag and drop analyses into projects
- View project overviews with all associated analyses
- Archive or delete projects when complete

## Progressive Disclosure

We've designed the interface to show you the most important information first, with details available when you need them.

### Expandable Sections
Throughout the platform, you'll find expandable sections that:
- Show summaries when collapsed
- Expand to reveal detailed information
- Remember your preferences for future visits
- Use keyboard shortcuts (Enter/Space) to toggle

### Tabbed Content
Complex information is organized into tabs:
- **Competitive Analysis**: Overview, Competitors, Market Position, Opportunities
- **Market Intelligence**: Demographics, Market Size, Trends
- Navigate with mouse clicks or arrow keys
- Swipe on mobile devices

### Accordions
Detailed sections use accordions to reduce scrolling:
- Only one section open at a time
- Click headers to expand/collapse
- Keyboard accessible

## Analysis Results

When you complete a gap analysis, results are presented progressively:

### Summary View (Always Visible)
- **Innovation Score**: Visual gauge showing opportunity potential
- **Feasibility Rating**: How realistic the opportunity is
- **Market Potential**: Size and growth indicators
- **Key Insights**: 3-5 bullet points highlighting main findings

### Detailed Sections (Expandable)
Click to expand any section for more details:
- **Competitive Analysis**: Deep dive into competitors and market positioning
- **Market Intelligence**: Demographics, market size, and trends
- **Detailed Insights**: Comprehensive analysis by category
- **Risk Assessment**: Potential challenges and mitigation strategies

### Action Plan
Your roadmap to bringing the opportunity to life:
- **4 Phases**: Validation, Planning, Development, Launch
- **Progress Tracking**: Check off completed steps
- **Phase Completion**: Celebrate milestones with animations
- **Progress Dashboard**: View completion across all projects

## Action Plan Progress Tracking

Stay motivated and organized as you work through your action plans.

### Checking Off Steps
- Click checkboxes next to each step to mark complete
- Progress updates automatically
- Undo accidental checks with a single click

### Phase Completion
When you complete all steps in a phase:
- Celebration animation appears
- Congratulatory message displays
- Next phase unlocks automatically

### Progress Dashboard
View your progress across all active projects:
- Overall completion percentages
- Phase-by-phase breakdown
- Filter by project
- Export progress reports

## Projects & Organization

### Creating Projects
1. Click "New Project" on your dashboard
2. Enter a project name and description
3. Add tags for easy filtering
4. Click "Create"

### Managing Projects
- **Add Analyses**: Drag and drop from recent searches or use the "Add to Project" button
- **Rename**: Click the edit icon on any project card
- **Archive**: Hide completed projects without deleting them
- **Delete**: Permanently remove projects (analyses remain in your history)

### Project Views
- **Overview**: See all analyses in a project at a glance
- **Progress**: Track action plan completion across the project
- **Export**: Generate combined reports for all analyses

## Sharing & Collaboration

### Creating Share Links
1. Open any analysis result
2. Click the "Share" button
3. Set an optional expiration date
4. Copy the generated link

### Share Link Features
- **Read-Only Access**: Recipients can view without signing up
- **Expiration Dates**: Set automatic link expiration
- **View Tracking**: See how many times your link was accessed
- **Revoke Access**: Disable links at any time

### Managing Shared Links
Access all your shared links from **Account > Shared Links**:
- View access statistics
- Update expiration dates
- Revoke access
- Create new links

## Contextual Help

Get help exactly when and where you need it.

### Tooltips
Hover over any UI element with an info icon to see:
- Brief explanations
- Keyboard shortcuts
- Quick tips

### Help Panel
Click the help icon (?) to open the contextual help panel:
- **Context-Aware**: Shows help relevant to your current page
- **Search**: Find specific help articles
- **Video Tutorials**: Watch feature demonstrations
- **FAQs**: Common questions and answers

### Help Shortcuts
- Press **?** to open keyboard shortcuts reference
- Press **Shift + ?** to open help panel
- Press **Esc** to close any help overlay

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Work faster with keyboard shortcuts for common actions.

### Global Shortcuts
- **Cmd/Ctrl + K**: Open global search
- **Cmd/Ctrl + N**: New search
- **Cmd/Ctrl + D**: Go to dashboard
- **?**: Show keyboard shortcuts reference
- **Shift + ?**: Open help panel
- **Esc**: Close modals and overlays

### Navigation Shortcuts
- **G then D**: Go to dashboard
- **G then S**: Go to search
- **G then R**: Go to resources
- **G then A**: Go to account

### Action Shortcuts
- **Cmd/Ctrl + E**: Export current analysis
- **Cmd/Ctrl + S**: Share current analysis
- **Cmd/Ctrl + F**: Toggle favorite

### Customizing Shortcuts
1. Go to **Account Settings > Keyboard Shortcuts**
2. Click on any shortcut to rebind
3. Press your desired key combination
4. Conflicts are automatically detected

## Global Search

Find anything across the platform instantly.

### Opening Global Search
- Press **Cmd/Ctrl + K**
- Click the search icon in the navigation
- Click in the search bar

### What You Can Search
- **Analyses**: Your gap analysis results
- **Resources**: Business tools and templates
- **Help Articles**: Documentation and guides
- **Pages**: Navigate directly to any page

### Search Features
- **Fuzzy Matching**: Finds results even with typos
- **Recent Searches**: Quick access to previous searches
- **Category Filtering**: Filter by type (analyses, resources, help, pages)
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Use arrow keys and Enter to navigate results

## Mobile Experience

Unbuilt is fully optimized for mobile devices.

### Mobile Navigation
- **Hamburger Menu**: Access all navigation from the menu icon

## Accessibility Features

Unbuilt is designed to be accessible to everyone.

### Keyboard Navigation
- Navigate with **Tab** and **Shift + Tab**
- Activate with **Enter** or **Space**
- Close with **Esc**
- **Skip to Main Content** link at the top of every page

### Screen Reader Support
- All images have descriptive alt text
- Charts include text descriptions
- ARIA labels on all interactive elements
- Live regions announce dynamic content

### Visual Accessibility
- **High Contrast Mode**: Toggle in accessibility settings
- **Color Contrast**: All text meets WCAG 2.1 AA standards (4.5:1 ratio)
- **No Color-Only Information**: Icons and text accompany all color coding
- **Resizable Text**: Zoom up to 200% without loss of functionality

### Accessibility Settings
Access from **Account Settings > Accessibility**:
- **High Contrast Mode**: Increase contrast for better visibility
- **Reduced Motion**: Disable animations and transitions
- **Screen Reader Mode**: Optimize for screen reader usage
- **Focus Indicators**: Enhanced focus visibility

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't Find a Feature**
- Use global search (Cmd/Ctrl + K) to find anything
- Check the help panel for guidance
- Review the interactive tour from settings

**Analysis Taking Too Long**
- Complex queries may take 30-60 seconds
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page if stuck over 2 minutes

**Share Link Not Working**
- Check if the link has expired
- Verify the link wasn't revoked
- Try generating a new link

**Progress Not Saving**
- Ensure you're logged in
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing and checking again

**Mobile Display Issues**
- Try rotating your device
- Clear browser cache
- Update to the latest browser version

**In-App Help**
- Press **?** for keyboard shortcuts

- Click help icons throughout the interface

**Feedback**

## Glossary

**Action Plan**: Step-by-step roadmap for bringing an opportunity to market

**Feasibility Rating**: Assessment of how realistic an opportunity is to pursue

**Gap Analysis**: Process of identifying what doesn't exist in a market

**Innovation Score**: Metric indicating the novelty and potential of an opportunity

**Market Intelligence**: Data about market size, demographics, and trends

**Progressive Disclosure**: Design pattern that reveals information gradually

**Project**: Collection of related gap analyses

**Share Link**: Secure URL for sharing analysis results without requiring login

**Tier**: Subscription level (Free, Pro, Enterprise)
