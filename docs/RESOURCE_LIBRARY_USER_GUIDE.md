## Getting Started

### Accessing the Resource Library

1. **From the Dashboard**: Click "Resources" in the main navigation menu
2. **From Action Plans**: Resources are automatically suggested within each action plan step
3. **From Search Results**: Access relevant resources directly from your gap analysis results

### Understanding Resource Types

The library contains five types of resources:

- **Tools**: Software, platforms, and services to help execute your ideas
- **Templates**: Pre-formatted documents you can customize (pitch decks, PRDs, legal docs)
- **Guides**: Step-by-step instructions and best practices
- **Videos**: Tutorial content and educational materials
- **Articles**: In-depth written content and case studies

### Resource Organization

Resources are organized by:

- **Phase**: Research, Validation, Development, Launch
- **Category**: Funding, Documentation, Marketing, Legal, Technical, Research
- **Idea Type**: Software, Physical Product, Service, Marketplace
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced

## Browsing Resources

### Using the Resource Library Page

1. **Navigate to Resources**: Click "Resources" in the main menu
2. **Browse by Category**: Use the category tabs to filter resources
3. **Filter by Phase**: Select which development phase you're in
4. **Sort Results**: Choose from:
   - Most Popular
   - Highest Rated
   - Recently Added
   - Most Relevant

### Search Functionality

**Basic Search**:
1. Enter keywords in the search bar
2. Results show matching resources with highlighted keywords
3. Relevance scores help identify the best matches

**Advanced Filtering**:
- **Category Filter**: Select one or more categories
- **Phase Filter**: Choose relevant development phases
- **Resource Type**: Filter by tool, template, guide, video, or article
- **Minimum Rating**: Show only highly-rated resources (4+ stars)
- **Premium Filter**: Toggle to show/hide premium resources

**Search Tips**:
- Use specific keywords related to your needs
- Combine filters for more precise results
- Try suggested searches based on your active projects
- Search within your bookmarks for quick access

### Resource Cards

Each resource card displays:
- **Title and Description**: What the resource is about
- **Category Badge**: Which category it belongs to
- **Phase Tags**: Relevant development phases
- **Rating**: Average user rating (1-5 stars)
- **Bookmark Icon**: Save for later access
- **View Count**: How many users have accessed it

## Contextual Suggestions

### Suggested Resources in Action Plans

When viewing your action plan, you'll see up to 3 relevant resources suggested for each step:

**How It Works**:
1. Resources are automatically matched to your current step
2. Matching considers:
   - Your development phase
   - Your idea type
   - Step keywords and context
   - Resource ratings and popularity
3. Click any resource to view details or access it directly

**Customizing Suggestions**:
- Click "Show more" to see additional resources
- Dismiss suggestions that aren't relevant
- Request different suggestions if needed

### Dashboard Recommendations

Your dashboard shows personalized recommendations based on:
- Your active gap analyses
- Your bookmarked resources
- Resources trending in your categories
- Your progress through action plans

## Bookmarking Resources

### Saving Resources

**To Bookmark a Resource**:
1. Click the bookmark icon on any resource card
2. The resource is instantly added to your collection
3. A confirmation appears showing it's been saved

**Managing Bookmarks**:
1. Navigate to "My Bookmarks" from your profile menu
2. View all saved resources organized by category
3. Add personal notes to any bookmarked resource
4. Add custom tags for better organization
5. Remove bookmarks you no longer need

### Adding Notes and Tags

**Personal Notes**:
- Click "Add Note" on any bookmarked resource
- Write reminders, insights, or implementation notes
- Notes are private and only visible to you
- Edit or delete notes anytime

**Custom Tags**:
- Create your own organizational system
- Add multiple tags per resource
- Filter bookmarks by your custom tags
- Examples: "urgent", "for-pitch", "team-review"

### Searching Bookmarks

Use the search bar in your bookmarks page to:
- Find resources by title or description
- Search within your personal notes
- Filter by category or custom tags
- Sort by date added or last accessed

## Rating and Reviewing

### Why Rate Resources?

Your ratings and reviews help:
- Other users find the most valuable resources
- The platform improve resource recommendations
- Administrators curate better content
- Contributors understand what works

### How to Rate a Resource

**After Using a Resource**:
1. You'll be prompted to rate it after accessing
2. Click the star rating (1-5 stars)
3. Optionally write a review
4. Submit your feedback

**Rating Guidelines**:
- ⭐ (1 star): Not helpful, poor quality
- ⭐⭐ (2 stars): Somewhat helpful, needs improvement
- ⭐⭐⭐ (3 stars): Helpful, meets expectations
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4 stars): Very helpful, high quality
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5 stars): Exceptional, highly recommend

### Writing Reviews

**Best Practices**:
- Be specific about what worked or didn't work
- Mention your use case and context
- Highlight unique features or benefits
- Keep it constructive and helpful
- Update your review if your opinion changes

**Review Display**:
- Reviews appear on the resource detail page
- Your name is shown (or anonymous if preferred)
- Other users can mark reviews as helpful
- Most helpful reviews appear first

### Editing Your Ratings

1. Go to the resource detail page
2. Find your existing rating
3. Click "Edit Rating"
4. Update your stars or review text
5. Save changes

## Contributing Resources

### Suggesting New Resources

**How to Contribute**:
1. Click "Suggest Resource" in the resource library
2. Fill out the contribution form:
   - **Title**: Clear, descriptive name
   - **Description**: What it does and why it's useful
   - **URL**: Link to the resource
   - **Category**: Select the best fit
   - **Tags**: Add relevant keywords
3. Submit for review

**Contribution Guidelines**:
- ✅ Resources should be publicly accessible
- ✅ Provide accurate, complete information
- ✅ Ensure the resource is high quality
- ✅ Check it's not already in the library
- ❌ No affiliate links without disclosure
- ❌ No spam or self-promotion
- ❌ No broken or malicious links

### Tracking Your Contributions

**View Contribution Status**:
1. Navigate to "My Contributions" from your profile
2. See all your submitted resources
3. Check status: Pending, Approved, or Rejected
4. Read admin feedback if rejected

**Contribution Statuses**:
- **Pending**: Under review by administrators
- **Approved**: Added to the library (you'll be credited!)
- **Rejected**: Not accepted (feedback provided)

### Editing Pending Contributions

While a contribution is pending:
1. Go to "My Contributions"
2. Click "Edit" on the pending item
3. Update any information
4. Resubmit for review

## Using Templates

### Finding Templates

Templates are marked with a "Template" badge and include:
- Pitch decks
- Product requirement documents (PRDs)
- Customer interview scripts
- Survey templates
- Legal documents (NDAs, founder agreements)
- Marketing plans
- Financial projections

### Generating Pre-Filled Templates

**For Template Resources**:
1. Click on a template resource
2. Click "Generate Template"
3. Select your gap analysis to use for pre-filling
4. Choose your preferred format:
   - Microsoft Word (.docx)
   - PDF
   - Google Docs
5. Download or open in your preferred application

**Template Variables**:
Templates automatically include:
- Your idea title and description
- Innovation score and feasibility rating
- Target market information
- Competitor analysis
- Key features and requirements
- Action plan summary

### Customizing Templates

After downloading:
1. Open in your preferred application
2. Review pre-filled sections
3. Customize with additional details
4. Add your branding and style
5. Save your customized version

## Notification Settings

### Opting In to Notifications

**Enable Resource Notifications**:
1. Go to Settings > Notifications
2. Toggle "Resource Updates" on
3. Customize your preferences:
   - Notification frequency (daily, weekly)
   - Categories of interest
   - Phases you're currently in
4. Save your settings

### What You'll Be Notified About

- New resources matching your interests
- Updates to bookmarked resources
- Responses to your contributions
- Trending resources in your categories
- Template updates and new formats

### Managing Notification Frequency

**Options**:
- **Real-time**: Immediate notifications for important updates
- **Daily Digest**: One email per day with all updates
- **Weekly Summary**: Weekly roundup of new resources
- **Off**: No resource notifications

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Are all resources free?**
A: Most resources are free for all users. Some premium resources require a Pro or Enterprise subscription.

**Q: Can I access resources offline?**
A: You can download templates and save resource links for offline reference. The library itself requires an internet connection.

**Q: How often are new resources added?**
A: New resources are added weekly. Enable notifications to stay updated.

**Q: Can I suggest resources from my own company?**
A: Yes, but clearly disclose any affiliation. Resources must provide genuine value to the community.

### Bookmarks and Organization

**Q: Is there a limit to how many resources I can bookmark?**
A: No, you can bookmark unlimited resources.

**Q: Can I share my bookmarked resources with team members?**
A: Currently, bookmarks are private. Team sharing features are coming in a future update.

**Q: Can I export my bookmarks?**
A: Yes, go to "My Bookmarks" and click "Export" to download a CSV file.

### Ratings and Reviews

**Q: Can I rate a resource without writing a review?**
A: Yes, reviews are optional. Star ratings alone are valuable.

**Q: Can I delete my review?**
A: Yes, you can edit or delete your reviews anytime from the resource detail page.

**Q: Are reviews moderated?**
A: Yes, reviews are monitored for spam and inappropriate content.

### Contributions

**Q: How long does review take?**
A: Most contributions are reviewed within 3-5 business days.

**Q: Why was my contribution rejected?**
A: Check the admin feedback in "My Contributions" for specific reasons. Common reasons include duplicate resources, broken links, or quality concerns.

**Q: Do I get credit for approved contributions?**
A: Yes! Your name appears as the contributor on approved resources.

**Q: Can I contribute multiple resources at once?**
A: You can submit up to 5 contributions per day.

### Templates

**Q: Do templates work with all gap analyses?**
A: Templates work best with complete gap analyses. Some fields may be empty if certain analysis sections weren't completed.

**Q: Can I customize template formats?**
A: You can choose from DOCX, PDF, or Google Docs formats. After downloading, customize as needed in your preferred application.

**Q: Are templates updated?**
A: Yes, templates are periodically updated. You'll be notified if a bookmarked template receives major updates.

### Technical Issues

**Q: A resource link is broken. What should I do?**
A: Click "Report Issue" on the resource page. Administrators will investigate and update or remove the resource.

**Q: Why aren't resources loading?**
A: Check your internet connection. If the issue persists, try clearing your browser cache or contact support.

**Q: Can I access resources on mobile?**
A: Yes, the resource library is fully responsive and works on all devices.
