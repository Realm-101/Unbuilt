# Interactive AI Conversations - Frequently Asked Questions

## Getting Started

### What are Interactive AI Conversations?

Interactive AI Conversations allow you to have follow-up discussions with the AI about your gap analysis. Instead of receiving a static report, you can ask questions, explore alternatives, and refine your analysis through natural conversation.

### How do I start a conversation?

After completing a gap analysis:
1. Scroll below your analysis results
2. You'll see the conversation interface with suggested questions
3. Click a suggested question or type your own in the input field
4. Press Enter to send

### Do I need to do anything special to enable conversations?

No, conversations are automatically available for all analyses. Just complete a gap analysis and the conversation interface appears below the results.

## Using Conversations

### What kind of questions can I ask?

You can ask about:
- Market validation and demand evidence
- Competitive analysis and threats
- Execution strategies and next steps
- Resource requirements and budgets
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Customer segments and targeting
- Business model alternatives
- Geographic market differences

### What questions should I avoid?

Avoid questions that are:
- Off-topic (not related to your business analysis)
- Requesting legal, medical, or financial advice
- Overly vague ("Tell me more")
- Asking for guarantees about success
- Inappropriate or offensive

### How does the AI understand my analysis context?

The AI has access to:
- Your original search query
- All identified gaps and scores
- Competitive analysis data
- Action plan phases
- Previous conversation history
- Modified parameters (for variants)

### Can I edit my questions after sending?

Yes, you can edit your messages within 5 minutes of sending. Click the edit icon on your message to make changes.

### Can I delete messages?

Yes, you can delete your own messages within 5 minutes. Click the delete icon and confirm. Note: This doesn't delete the AI's response to that message.

## Suggested Questions

### What are suggested questions?

Suggested questions are AI-generated follow-up questions tailored to your specific analysis. They help you discover insights you might not have thought to ask about.

### How are suggested questions generated?

The AI analyzes:
- Your gap analysis results
- Identified opportunities and challenges
- Your conversation history
- Knowledge gaps in the analysis
- Common user concerns

### Do suggested questions change?

Yes! After each exchange, the AI generates new suggested questions based on the conversation flow and what you've already discussed.

### Can I refresh suggested questions?

Yes, click the "Refresh Suggestions" button to generate new questions if the current ones don't interest you.

### Why are suggested questions categorized?

Categories help you navigate different aspects of your analysis:
- **Market Validation**: Demand and timing questions
- **Competitive Analysis**: Competitor and positioning questions
- **Execution Strategy**: Implementation and resource questions
- **Risk Assessment**: Challenge and mitigation questions

## Tier Limits

### How many questions can I ask on the Free tier?

Free tier users can ask:
- 5 questions per analysis
- 20 questions per day total
- No analysis variants

### What do I get with Pro tier?

Pro tier includes:
- Unlimited questions per analysis
- Unlimited daily questions
- Up to 5 analysis variants
- 1,000 character message limit (vs 500 for Free)
- Real-time streaming responses
- Priority support

### What happens when I reach my question limit?

You'll see a message indicating you've reached your limit with options to:
- Upgrade to Pro for unlimited questions
- Wait for daily reset (midnight UTC)
- Start a new analysis (resets per-analysis limit)

### Do conversations count toward my search limit?

No, conversations are separate from your initial gap analysis searches. Your search limit only applies to creating new analyses.

### If I upgrade mid-conversation, can I continue immediately?

Yes! Your upgrade takes effect immediately, and you can continue asking questions without waiting.

## Analysis Variants

### What are analysis variants?

Variants are alternative versions of your analysis based on modified assumptions. For example, analyzing the same idea for a different market, customer segment, or business model.

### How do I create a variant?

Simply ask a "what if" question:
- "What if I targeted small businesses instead of enterprises?"
- "What if I used a subscription model?"
- "What if I focused on the European market?"

The AI detects the refinement request and offers to create a variant.

### How many variants can I create?

- Free tier: 0 variants
- Pro tier: Up to 5 variants per analysis
- Enterprise tier: Unlimited variants

### Can I compare variants?

Yes! Use the variant selector to:
- Switch between original and variants
- View side-by-side comparisons
- See modified parameters
- Compare innovation scores and gaps

### Do variants have separate conversations?

Yes, each variant has its own conversation thread. This keeps discussions focused and organized.

### Can I delete variants?

Yes, you can delete variants you no longer need. The original analysis is always preserved.

## Response Quality

### How accurate are AI responses?

The AI provides responses based on:
- Your specific analysis data
- Market research and trends
- Business best practices
- Logical reasoning

However, AI responses should be used as guidance, not absolute truth. Always validate critical decisions with additional research.

### What if the AI gives an incorrect response?

You can:
- Point out the error in a follow-up question
- Rate the response with thumbs down
- Report the response if it's significantly wrong
- Contact support for persistent issues

### Why does the AI include disclaimers?

Disclaimers appear for:
- Financial projections (not financial advice)
- Legal considerations (not legal advice)
- Success predictions (no guarantees)
- Assumptions made in responses

This ensures you understand the limitations and use responses appropriately.

### Can the AI provide sources for its claims?

Yes, when making specific claims about markets, competitors, or trends, the AI cites sources when available. Look for source citations in responses.

### What does "confidence level" mean?

Confidence indicators show how certain the AI is:
- **High**: Based on clear data from your analysis
- **Medium**: Reasonable assumptions made
- **Low**: Speculative or uncertain

Staff can only access conversations:

### Can I delete my conversation data?

Yes, you can:
- Clear individual conversations
- Delete specific analyses (including conversations)

### Are conversations used to train AI models?

No, your conversations are not used to train AI models. They're only used to provide you with contextual responses.

## Troubleshooting

### "Rate limit exceeded" error

**Cause**: Reached question limit for your tier  
**Solution**: 
- Upgrade to Pro for unlimited questions
- Wait for daily reset (midnight UTC)
- Start a new analysis (resets per-analysis limit)

### "AI service error" message

**Cause**: Temporary AI service issue  
**Solution**:
- Wait 30 seconds and try again
- System auto-retries 3 times
- Contact support if persists

### "Context too large" warning

**Cause**: Conversation became very long  
**Solution**:
- AI automatically summarizes older messages
- Or clear conversation and start fresh
- This is handled automatically in most cases

### Response seems irrelevant

**Cause**: Question may be too vague or off-topic  
**Solution**:
- Rephrase with more specific context
- Reference specific parts of your analysis
- Break complex questions into smaller ones

### Suggested questions not appearing

**Cause**: Loading delay or technical issue  
**Solution**:
- Wait a few seconds for generation
- Refresh the page
- Click "Refresh Suggestions" button
- Contact support if persists

### Cannot edit or delete message

**Cause**: 5-minute edit window expired  
**Solution**:
- Send a new message clarifying or correcting
- Or clear conversation and start over

### Streaming response stops mid-sentence

**Cause**: Network interruption  
**Solution**:
- Check internet connection
- Refresh page to see complete response
- System saves partial responses

## Best Practices

### How can I get better responses?

1. **Be Specific**: Include constraints, budgets, timelines
2. **Provide Context**: Reference specific analysis sections
3. **Ask Follow-Ups**: Dig deeper into interesting points
4. **Use Variants**: Explore different scenarios
5. **Build on Previous Answers**: Create a logical flow

### What's the best conversation flow?

A good flow typically:
1. Starts with market validation questions
2. Explores competitive landscape
3. Dives into execution strategy
4. Addresses specific concerns
5. Creates variants for alternatives

### How do I organize long conversations?

- Use clear conversation to reset when changing topics
- Create variants for different scenarios
- Export important conversations for reference
- Use search to find specific topics

### Should I ask multiple questions at once?

It's better to ask one focused question at a time. This allows:
- More detailed responses
- Better conversation flow
- Easier to follow up
- Clearer context for AI
